The winner of Femina Miss India will represent India at Miss World 2019 in London, United Kingdom.

Miss World 2019, will be the 69th edition of the Miss World, it will be held on December 14, 2019 at the ExCeL London in London, United Kingdom. Vanessa Ponce of Mexico will crown her successor at the end of the event. The official announcement was made on February 19, 2019, in Bangkok, with the presence of Miss World President Julia Morley and Tanawat Wansom, Chief Executive Officer of TW Pageants. Mr. Wansom along with chief guest Ashwani Kumar Rai announced a mid-December planned date for the contest with the final venue yet to be announced officially.

It will be held at Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel Indoor Stadium, Mumbai on June 15, 2019

Femina Miss India 2019 was the 56th edition of the Femina Miss India beauty pageant. It was held at Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel Indoor Stadium, Mumbai on June 15, 2019, and was hosted by Karan Johar, Vickey Kaushal and Miss World 2017, Manushi Chhillar.
At the end of the event, Anukreethy Vas of Tamil Nadu crowned Miss India 2019 and will represent India at Miss World 2019 in London.
There are four regions under which the contestants are grouped - North, South, East and West.
Mentors will train contestants for the finale. All the Schedule is given below.
